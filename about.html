FAQ

What color is Mercury?

Mercury is a dark gray color, covered in craters of several sizes, with the ocassional light patch.

Can you breath on Mercury?

No, the atmosphere is too thin. Mercury Travel provides equipment for a comfortable stay on Mercury.

Can you jump really high?

Mercury has about 38% of the gravity on Earth, meaning you can jump really high.

What is there to do on Mercury?

Mercury is the best planet in the solar system for jumping around and having fun on the surface, and you can visit craters to drive around in our rovers.

What is the weather like?

Mercury gets very cold and very hot, we provide everything necessary for your trip to Mercury.

What is the best place to visit?

Any of the large craters are very fun to visit with a Mercury Travel Rover.

Do you sell souvenirs?

We sell many pictures of the planet and rocks from Mercury, carved to look like Mercury.

Do people live there?

While Mercury is an excellent place to travel, nobody lives here due to the harsh conditions.

Is there vegetation?

No, nothing lives on Mercury.

What do I bring?

Anything you want if it's legal of course.
